## [不存在的两种情况](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=1247.3&p=13)

> 极限结果为无穷

$$
判断数列 \{2^n\} 是否收敛 \\
\lim_{n\to\infty} 2^n = \infty \quad \text{因此}\quad \{2^n\} \text{发散}
$$

> 极限值在某个范围内波动

$$
判断数列 \{(-1)^n\} 是否收敛\\
\lim_{n\to\infty} (-1)^n = \begin{cases} 1 & n\text{为偶数} \\ -1 & n\text{为奇数} \end{cases} \quad \text{因此}\quad \{(-1)^n\} \text{发散}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{n\to\infty} \left( \frac{1}{1\times2} + \frac{1}{2\times3} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n(n+1)} \right)
&= \lim_{n\to\infty} \left( 1 - \frac12 + \frac12 - \frac13 + \cdots + \frac1n - \frac1{n+1} \right) \\
&= \lim_{n\to\infty} \left(1 - \frac{1}{n+1}\right) \\
&= 1
\end{aligned}
$$

### [题目四](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=2315.8&p=13)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{n\to\infty} \left(\sqrt{n+2} - \sqrt{n}\right)
&= \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{(\sqrt{n+2}-\sqrt{n})(\sqrt{n+2}+\sqrt{n})}{\sqrt{n+2}+\sqrt{n}} \\
&= \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{n+2-n}{\sqrt{n+2}+\sqrt{n}} \\
&= \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{2}{\sqrt{n+2}+\sqrt{n}} \\
&= 0
\end{aligned}
$$

## [趋近于无穷](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=372.6&p=14)

> 一个函数要存在极限，那么他的正负无穷需要为同一个值

函数在无穷处极限存在的充要条件:

$$
\lim_{x\to\infty}f(x) = A \iff \lim_{x\to+\infty}f(x) = \lim_{x\to-\infty}f(x) = A
$$

$$
\text{若 } \lim_{x\to-\infty}f(x) \neq \lim_{x\to+\infty}f(x) \text{，则 } \lim_{x\to\infty}f(x) \text{ 不存在}
$$

### 第三题

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text{判断 } \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 + e^\frac{1}{x}}{2 + e^\frac{2}{x}} \text{ 极限是否存在} \\
&\text{左: } \lim_{x \to 0^-} \frac{1 + e^\frac{1}{x}}{2 + e^\frac{2}{x}} = \frac{1}{2} \\
&\text{右: } \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{1 + e^{1/x}}{2 + (e^2)^{1/x}} \\
&= \frac{e}{e^2} \\
&= \text{上大无穷，下大0，因此为 } 0
\end{aligned}
$$

# 两个重要极限

## 第一个重要极限

$$
设当 x \to 0 时，\varphi(x) \to 0，且 \varphi(x) \sim x (等价) \\
则极限 \lim_{x\to0}\frac{\varphi(x)}{x}=1 为 \frac{0}{0} 型。
$$

> 人话: 不管x趋于多少，只要分子和分母的形式一致，并且极限等于0，最终结果一定为1。

$$
第一重要极限\frac{0}{0}: \lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin{x}}{x}=1
$$

> 1的无穷次方使用万能方法

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 0} (\frac{1+x}{1+\sin x}) ^ {\frac{1}{x^3}} \\
& 定型: 1^\infty 使用万能方法 (1+A)^B = e^{\lim{AB}} \\
& => (1 + \frac{1+x}{1+\sin x} - 1) ^ {\frac{1}{x^3}} \\
& => (1 + \frac{1+x}{1+\sin x} - \frac{1 + \sin x}{1 + \sin x}) ^ {\frac{1} {x^3}} \\
& => (1 + \frac{(1+x) - (1+\sin x) => 1+x-1-\sin x}{1+\sin x}) ^ {\frac{1} {x^3}} \\
& => (\frac{x-\sin x}{1+\sin x})^ {\frac{1} {x^3}} \\
& => e^{\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x - \sin x}{ (1 + \sin x)x^3 }} \\
& 使用非零因子代入 \\
& => \frac{x-\sin x}{x^3} \\
& 使用x - \sin x => \frac{1}{6} x^3 \\
& => e^{\frac{1}{6}}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}

& \text{则(分子分母同时求导): } \lim_{x\to \sdot}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x\to \sdot}\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)} 
\end{aligned}
$$

## [题目一](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=748&p=24)

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1 - x}{x^2} \\
\text{分步求导: } \\
& e^x \to e^x \ln e \to e^x \sdot 1 \to e^x \\
& 1 \to 0 \\
& x \to x^1 \to 1 \sdot x^{1-1} \to x^0 \to 1 \\
& x^2 \to 2 \sdot x^{2-1} \to 2 \sdot x \to 2x \\
\text{代入:} \\
& \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 0 - 1}{2x} \to \frac{e^x-1}{2x} \\
\text{使用: }&e^x - 1 = x \\
& \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x}{2x} \to \frac{1}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 0比0

> 约分，非零因子代入，有理化，等价无穷小，洛必达

### [第一题](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=155&p=25)

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 -3x +2}{x^2+2x-3} \\
\text{因式分解: } & \frac{(x-1)(x-2)}{(x+3)(x-1)} \\
=> & \frac{x-2}{x+3} \to \frac{-1}{4} \\
=> & -\frac{1}{4}
\end{aligned}
$$

答案: - 1/4

### [第二题](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=334&p=25)

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{\arctan x} \\
\text{方案1: } & \text{使用等价无穷小} \arctan x = x \\ 
=> & \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{x} \to \frac{e^{-x}(e^{2x} - 1)}{x} \\
& \text{使用非零因子替换 + 等价无穷小} e^{-0}=1 \\
=> & \frac{1 \sdot 2x}{x} \to \frac{2x}{x} \\
=> & 2 \\
\text{方案2: } & \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{x} \\
=> & \frac{e^x + 1 - e^{-x} - 1}{x} \to \frac{(e^x + 1) - (e^{-x} + 1) }{x} \\
=> & \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{(e^x + 1)}{x} - \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{ (e^{-x} + 1)}{x} \\
& \text{使用等价无穷小} e^x + 1 = x \\
=> & \frac{x}{x} - \frac{-x}{x} \\
=> & 1 - (-1) \to 1 + 1 \\
=> & 2
\end{aligned}
$$

答案: 2

### [第三题](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=750&p=25)

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2 - 4}{\sqrt{x} - \sqrt{2}} \\
\text{根式有理化: } & \frac{(x^2-4)(\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{2})}{(\sqrt{x} - \sqrt{2})(\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{2})} \\
=> & \frac{(x^2-4)(\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{2})}{x+2} \\
\text{因式分解: } & \frac{ (x-2)(x+2)(\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{2}) } {x+2} \\
=> & (x-2)(\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{2}) \\
=> & 4(2\sqrt{2}) \\
=> & 8\sqrt{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

答案: 8根号2

\end{aligned}

### [第五题](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=1121&p=25)

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{3\sin x + x^2 cos \frac{1}{x}}{(1+\cos x ) \ln(1+x)} \\
\text{使用非零因子带入: } & 1 + \cos x \to 2  \\
\text{使用等价无穷小: } & \ln(1+x) \to x \\
=> & \frac{3\sin x + x^2 \cos \frac{1}{x}}{2x} \\
\text{使用极限的四则运算: } & \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{3\sin x}{2x} + \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^2\cos\frac{1}{x} }{2x} \\
=> & \frac{3}{2} + \text{约一个x，把二提上去} \\
=> & \frac{3}{2} + \frac{1}{2}x\cos \frac{1}{x} \\
\text{零乘有界等于零}: &\frac{3}{2} + 0 \to \frac{3}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### [第六题](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1husGzwEtZ?t=1336&p=25)

\begin{aligned}

= & \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-\frac{1}{2}x^2}{x^2} = -\frac{1}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
